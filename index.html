The term "Load Shedding' referS to suspension of electricity on certain lines for short or long time. Load shedding happens when the demand is greater than the generation. There are many reasons for load shedding. The insufficient production of electricity is the main reason of it. Misuse and illegal connection of electricity are the other reasons of it. It occurs mostly at night because the demand of electricity is more than in the daytime. The bad effects of load shedding are beyond description. Load shedding causes unbearable torture on the people belonging to ail walks of life especially during summer. But the sufferings of the parents and the students beggar description. Students sit idle in the dark closing their books especially before their exam. Domestic life becomes painiul. The housewives grope in the darkness in the kitchen. Mills and factories come to a Standstill incurring huge amount of national loss. So, load shedding hampers productivity. Besides, shops, markets and other establishments become seriously affected by it. In addition, due to load Snedding foods kept in the refrigerator get rotten and the commodities preserved in the cold Storages get spoilt. It is very difficult to solve this problem. The concerned authority must take iccessary steps to stop it. More power stations should be established. Illegal connection and ystem loss should also be stopped. The sooner the problem is solved. the better for bath individual and national life.
